Prompt Manager offers two plans tailored to your needs.

## FREE Plan

Ideal for discovering the platform and for personal projects.

**Included:**
- Unlimited prompt management (creation, editing).
- Full versioning.
- Access to free models.

**Limits:**
- **3 Executions / day** (Via UI or API).
- No BYOK functionality.

## PRO Plan ($7 / month)

For professionals and teams who need power and flexibility.

**Benefits:**
- **Unlimited Executions**: No more daily quotas on the interface.
- **Priority Access**: Priority customer support.
- **Premium Models**: Access to the most capable models (GPT-4, Opus, etc.).
- **BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)**: Connect your own OpenRouter account.

### BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)

BYOK allows PRO users to use their own OpenRouter API key.

**Why?**
- **Privacy**: You control your data.
- **Real Costs**: You pay the provider (OpenRouter) directly at cost price, with no surcharge.
- **No Limits**: You are only limited by your OpenRouter budget.

**Configuration:**
1. Go to **Settings > BYOK**.
2. Enter your OpenRouter key (`sk-or-...`).
3. Save.
